To ensure your AI output moves beyond simple generation into genuine artistry, you need to incorporate techniques used by the historical masters. We look specifically for dramatic lighting and rich texture.

1.  **Chiaroscuro:** Extreme contrasts between light and dark (dramatic shadows).
2.  **Textural Detail:** Hyper-realistic brushstrokes that mimic canvas texture.
3.  **Formal Attire:** A dark, period-appropriate suit or waistcoat.
