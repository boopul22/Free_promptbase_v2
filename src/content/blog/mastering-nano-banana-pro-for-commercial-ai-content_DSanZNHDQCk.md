## How to Use Nano Banana Pro for Consistent Imagery

The biggest challenge with AI is often "the lottery effect"—you get something different every time. Here is a **Nano Banana Pro tutorial** on how to maintain consistency.

### Setting Up Your Brand-Specific Workspace
Start by uploading your brand’s color palette and existing photography style. The Pro version allows you to create a "Brand DNA" profile. This acts as a set of guardrails for the AI, ensuring it doesn't wander off-brand.

### Defining Core Product Styles
Consistency starts with the prompt. Instead of saying "a bottle," you must describe the material (frosted glass), the lighting (soft morning sun), and the angle (eye-level).

### Utilizing Pro Features for Style Replication
One of the best **AI image consistency tips** is using the "Style Reference" tool. By feeding the AI one "hero" image, you can instruct Nano Banana Pro to match the shadows, highlights, and grit of that specific photo in every subsequent generation.

### Implementing Multi-Image Campaigns
When creating a series, use "seed" numbers. Keeping the same seed allows you to make minor adjustments to a scene without changing the entire composition. This is how you create a "family" of images that look like they were shot on the same day.

1.  **Refine for Social Media:** Crop your images appropriately for 9:16 (Stories) or 4:5 (Feeds). Don't just post what the AI spits out.
2.  **Avoid Artifacts:** Look closely at hands, text, or reflections. If something looks "off," use the "In-painting" tool in Nano Banana Pro to fix those specific areas.
3.  **Balance Automation and Creativity:** Use AI to do the heavy lifting (the background, the lighting), but keep the human in charge of the "soul" of the image—the story it tells.
