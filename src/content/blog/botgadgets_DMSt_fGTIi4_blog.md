## The Prompt Formula That Delivers Instant Magic

The beauty of this system is that once you have the foundation, you can tweak the **[animal]** name and instantly create dozens of unique, viral-ready images.

We used a combination of ChatGPT to refine the concept and an AI art generator (like Midjourney or Leonardo AI) to execute the prompt. The key is in the descriptive language that focuses the AI on realism, texture, and scale.

Here is the exact prompt structure we used:

![Step-by-step guide showing how to use ChatGPT and a structured prompt to create ultra-realistic fingertip animal art](https://pub-141831e61e69445289222976a15b6fb3.r2.dev/insta_post/DMSt_fGTIi4/image_8.jpg)

<prompt>Create an ultra-realistic digital illustration of an unbelievably tiny and adorable baby [animal] sitting gently on the tip of a human finger. It has soft, fluffy fur, round sparkling eyes, and a calm, curious expression. The background should be a smooth, softly blurred warm brown tone. Lighting is soft and warm, enhancing the cuteness and realism. Show highly detailed textures on both the [animal]'s fur and the human skin. Format: vertical 9:16, perfect for instagram or mobile wallpaper.</prompt>
