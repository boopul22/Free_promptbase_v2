## The Structure Behind the Style

Achieving this high-fidelity look requires more than just listing keywords. It requires structure, specific modifiers, and an understanding of how tools like Leonardo AI and Midjourney interpret stylistic cues.

When crafting a prompt, we move beyond simple subject description and focus on technical details like lighting, camera angle, and finishing style. The inclusion of parameters like `--ar 2:3` (aspect ratio) ensures the composition feels intentional, not accidental.

The goal is to provide a complete visual blueprint that leaves zero room for artistic interpretation by the machine.
