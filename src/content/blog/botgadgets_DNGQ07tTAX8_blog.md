## The Key to Success: Mastering the ChatGPT Prompt

The secret sauce behind this #hyperrealism isn't just the AI model (we rely heavily on the capabilities introduced in #chatgpt5); it’s the structure of the prompt itself. These aren't just simple descriptions—they are detailed, cinematic scripts written specifically for the AI’s visual engine.

We ensure every detail is included, specifying:
1. The ingredients and composition.
2. The action (splashing, swirling, hovering).
3. The atmosphere (light-filled kitchen, diffused daylight).
4. The technical specifications (4K studio lighting, shallow depth of field, aspect ratios).

This level of precision is crucial for moving past generic AI art into the realm of custom, editorial-style #foodart. Below is an example of the kind of detailed prompt needed to achieve these results. Notice how the lighting, background elements, and specific textural instructions are layered in.
