## Section 3: The Wong Kar-wai Cinematic Style

A highly sought-after aesthetic is the moody, neon-drenched world of Wong Kar-wai. When replicating this, you must specify the **color palette** (red-green), the **atmosphere** (rain, neon reflections), and the **camera artifacts** (film grain, motion blur).

This is about atmosphere and lighting more than clothes:

![Wong Kar-wai style portrait inside an old taxi with red and green neon light reflecting on the face](https://pub-141831e61e69445289222976a15b6fb3.r2.dev/insta_post/DO-fIaUjCiF/image_6.jpg)

Slightly relaxed. Hair is wet. Image tone: Contrast red-green. Film grain thick. Motion blur clear. Glow blur around car light. Mood: Lonely, solitary, big city with no answers.</prompt>
