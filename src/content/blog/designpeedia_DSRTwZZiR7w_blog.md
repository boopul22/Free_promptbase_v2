## How to implement ethically and responsibly

Ethics aren‚Äôt optional here. They‚Äôre the backbone that keeps your brand trusted and compliant. A thoughtful approach to consent, transparency, frequency, and platform policy will protect both you and your audience.

### 4.1 Consent and opt-in

Always make opt-in explicit. If you‚Äôre asking someone to comment to receive a DM, you‚Äôre prompting them to join a private conversation. ### 4.2 Clear value proposition

Your audience should know what they‚Äôll get in return for commenting X‚ÄôMas. It could be a discount code, an exclusive guide, or early access to a product drop. ### 4.3 Frequency and sequencing

Don‚Äôt overdo it. A single well-timed DM per commenter is usually enough. For higher-engagement audiences, you can follow up with one additional message, but keep tiers and sequencing intentional. If a follower ignores the DM, respect their boundaries and avoid a flood of follow-ups.

Every platform has rules about messages, promotions, and automation. Stay within those boundaries. Regularly review platform policies and adjust your prompts to stay compliant while still being effective.
