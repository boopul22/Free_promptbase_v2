## Tips for Getting the Best Results with Your Prompts

To make your **AI photo transformation** look as real as possible, keep these pro-tips in mind:

*   **Choosing a High-Quality Source Photo:** Avoid blurry or pixelated selfies. The more detail the AI can see in your eyes and hair, the better the final "Stranger Things" version will look.
*   **Understanding Light and Shadow:** If your original photo is bright and sunny, the AI has to work harder to make it "dark and spooky." Try using a photo taken in a room with a single lamp or during "golden hour" for easier transitions.
*   **Simple Tweaks:** Don't be afraid to add your own words to the prompt. If you want to wear a specific color or be in a specific location (like a forest or a laboratory), just add that text to the end of the **easy AI art prompts**.
