2.  **Navigating to the Nano Banana Toolset:** Look for the image generation or "Vision" tab. Depending on your specific setup, you may need to toggle on "Reference Mode" or "Character Consistency" features.
3.  **Setting Up Your Workspace:** Keep your windows organized. Have a folder on your desktop with your "Source" face (you) and your "Target" style (the vibe you want).
