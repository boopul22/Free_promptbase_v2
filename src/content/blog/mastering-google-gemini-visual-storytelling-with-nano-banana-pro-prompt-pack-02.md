## Mastering Continuity and Camera Angles

**Character consistency** is the biggest headache for AI creators. Keeping a face and outfit identical across multiple prompts is usually a nightmare. 

With "Composite Prompting," you can generate a 3x3 contact sheet in one go. Calling for a specific grid forces Gemini to render the same subject from nine perspectives while locking lighting and wardrobe. This is the fastest way to build a believable character profile.

## Multiverses and Identity Locking

The "Identity Lock" lets you drop one person into different "multiverses" without losing their soul. You can render a character as a human, an anime protagonist, and a 3D hero in one frame while maintaining the core face.

"Temporal Transformations" allow for age progression while keeping bone structure consistent. This control turns Gemini from a toy into a production tool.
